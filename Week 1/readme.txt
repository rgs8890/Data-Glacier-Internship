Agile Methodology: Hardly a competetive advantage, Agile is a way of thinking/ working adapting to changing requirements and feedback typically involving deploying tools like Jira, Azure DevOps and Digital.AI.
Scrum: This is an Agile Framework Methodology involving Sprints with a Scrum Master, Product Owner and Development Team and a Backlog of Tasks which are reviewed after every Sprint and updated.
Kanban: Another Agile Framework Methodology; this is more flexible than Scrum and is focused on breaking the project down into small tasks presented on a Kanban Board as Kanban Cards which are in constant flow, always moving to the write. A typical board will look like this:
To Do  || In Completion || Done || Review
